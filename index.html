FAQs

A URL shortener is a tool that takes a long URL and creates a shortened version, making it easier to share on social media, in emails, or anywhere with character limitations.

Our URL shortener works by taking your long URL and generating a unique, shortened link. When someone clicks on the shortened link, they are redirected to the original long URL.

Shortened URLs created with our service have no expiration date. They will remain active as long as our URL shortening service is operational.
